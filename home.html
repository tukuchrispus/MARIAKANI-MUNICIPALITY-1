I Salute the People of Mariakani municipality and the Nation Of Kenya.And Welcome you to Mariakani Municipality. Mariakani Municipality is embraced by lush greenery and a thriving community spirit, Mariakani Municipality invites you to discover its charm and potential. Strategically located between Mombasa and Nairobi, Mariakani serves as a pivotal junction for trade and development. With its rich agricultural heritage, diverse cultural tapestry, and burgeoning industries, Mariakani offers a promising environment for growth and prosperity, making it an ideal destination for both residents and investors.

 

MISSION STATEMENT
Our mission is to enhance the quality of life for all residents by providing efficient and inclusive governance, fostering economic growth, preserving our natural and cultural heritage, and promoting a safe, vibrant, and sustainable community.
